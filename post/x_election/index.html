Do Alice Weidel and the AfD benefit from Musk's attention to X?

Sami Nenno, Philipp Lorenz-Spreen

Did Elon Musk manipulate X to give the AfD more reach? He has made it clear that he is a fan in posts, articles and election events – and at the same time, Alice Weidel’s reach on X increased significantly. Are these developments linked? This blog post describes a data analysis in which we take a closer look at which accounts received how much reach and whether Musk’s influence can be determined. Where does the sudden increase in Weidel’s reach on X come from?
